What is Bet4Gain?

Bet4Gain is a production-ready, real-time multiplayer crash/aviator betting game. Players place bets before a round starts, then watch an exponentially growing multiplier on a canvas-animated curve. The goal is to cash out before the multiplier "crashes". Every round is provably fair using HMAC-SHA256 cryptography, and the entire experience is synchronized in real-time across all connected players via WebSockets.
